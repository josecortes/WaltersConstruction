Project Description


This site is meant for the use of employees and owners of a contruction company. The employees work in a widely dispersed area and do not go to a central work site. The site is used for employees to enter their work hours, and for owners to pull the hours worked information. The site can also be used for users to send messages to each other and to allow management to send broadcast messages to all employees. In its current state, the initial log in screen works and redirects users to a home page for owners and a home page for employees. The database functionality is there for log in purposes. There are additional pages for creating/changing users in the database, but these pages should be restricted to proper users in the production release. Many tests have been created, and about 20 of them do not pass at this time because the site is not yet complete.

Minimum Site Requirements


Minimum requirements for this site to be useful are: 1) Logins are correctly processed (This is working) 2) Users have appropriate home pages (This is working) 3) Employees are able to enter their hours worked, and run hours worked reports for themselves (Not done) 4) Owners can pull hours worked reports for employees (Not Done)
